# Product Vision

## Goal

The goal of this software is to build a streamlined, mobile-friendly contractor management system specifically designed for Refine Physio Mobile.

As the business grows, managing mobile physiotherapy, occupational therapy, and remedial massage services through multiple systems is becoming inefficient and time-consuming. The aim of this software is to centralise referrals, appointments, contractor schedules, treatment notes, reports, and client management into one simple platform that integrates directly with Cliniko.

## Intended Outcomes

The software is intended to:

- Reduce admin workload and double handling
- Improve organisation and communication between admin and contractors
- Simplify mobile appointment management
- Streamline report writing and documentation
- Improve visibility of contractor schedules and availability
- Ensure contractors only access their own clients and bookings
- Create a smoother workflow from referral through to treatment and reporting
- Allow the business to scale efficiently as more mobile contractors join the team

## Product Principles

A major focus of the platform is simplicity and usability for contractors working on the road. The system should be fast, easy to use on phones, and designed around real mobile allied health workflows.

The platform should support admin/reception and contractor workflows without recreating Cliniko. Cliniko should remain the source of truth for real patients, practitioners, calendars, and bookings, while this software supports the mobile-specific workflow around referrals, rebooking, notes, reports, approvals, and operational visibility.

## Long-Term Vision

The long-term vision is to create a scalable internal platform that supports the continued growth of Refine Physio Mobile while maintaining high-quality client care, professional documentation, and efficient operational systems.
